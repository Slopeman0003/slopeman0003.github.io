The Galt House is a 25-story, 1300-room hotel established in 1972 located in Louisville, Kentucky. It is named for a nearby historic hotel that was erected in the year 1837 and demolished in the year 1921. The Galt House is the city's only hotel on the Ohio River. Here you can immerse yourself in the history of Kentucky's native spirit with the nearby Whiskey Row and Museum Row, featuring icons such as Muhammad Ali and the Louisville Slugger. The Galt House Hotel is in the center of it all.
